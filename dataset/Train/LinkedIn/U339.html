        Background
        Summary
        I’ve spent my entire professional career in the fields of domestic and international marketing and business development. My work experience spans from large corporations to small businesses; private and public organizations; as well as non-profit and for-profit companies.
        

        
After earning my undergraduate degree in international marketing, I started my professional career as an international sports marketing executive, developing international marketing campaigns and public relations programs for professional baseball teams and athletes.
        

        
I attended the University of Nebraska on a full ride scholarship and earned my MBA. Before graduating, I attended the prestigious Focus Leadership Institute in Colorado Springs, Colorado. After graduating from the University of Nebraska, I moved to Colorado to work as an international media executive, where I developed international marketing campaigns to expand the organization’s Spanish media network worldwide.
        

        
After five years, I moved from Colorado to Texas to be in charge of the international department at RPS International. My main duty was to lead the expansion of the company’s worldwide distributor network and the company's global marketing initiatives. I tripled the company’s international presence; expanded the distributors’ network in the Middle East, Europe and Mexico; and introduced the brand to Africa, Asia, Central America and the Caribbean. 
        

        
I am a CGBP (Certified Global Business Professional), CBC (Certified Business Consultant), and ECoP (Export Compliance Professional), and currently work at UTSA’s International Trade Center as a Sr. International Business Consultant. 
        

        
I also enjoy public speaking and have been guest speaker and keynote speaker at international trade, marketing and business seminars and universities in the U.S. and Mexico.
