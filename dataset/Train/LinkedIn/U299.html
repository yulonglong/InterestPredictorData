        Background
        Summary
        I am a wine & spirits educator and industry consultant with over 14 years of experience in the field. 
        

        
I am a long-time instructor at the prestigious International Wine Center, where I teach the courses of the Wine & Spirits Education Trust, in addition to collaborating with a wide range of support organizations such as trade offices and public relations agencies. 
        

        
Since April 2009 I have been the Armagnac Ambassador to New York and have been educating both the trade and consumers on the diverse pleasures of France's oldest spirits. 
        

        
I have also been contracted, on the behalf of both the Champagne and the Port regions, through the Center of Wine Origins, as their NY Ambassador, to spread the 'location matters' message. 
        

        
I have taught classes for consumer groups at established NY institutions such as Artisanal Cheese Center and Astor Center, as well as presented lectures to large trade organizations at national conferences such as the Society of Wine Educators and Tales of the Cocktails. 
        

        
Nothing gives me more pleasure than sharing my passion of wine & spirits with others.
        

        
Specialties: Wine Education, Wine Tasting Technique, Champagne, Port Wine, Sherry Wine, Armagnac, French Wines, Food & Wine Pairing, Cheese & Wine Pairing, Consumer Wine Tasting Events, Trade-Focused Presentation
