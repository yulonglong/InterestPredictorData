        Background
        Summary
        I lead two companies that provide agency support to organisations to improve their marketing and communications through the development of excellent digital content. These are:
        

        
- Pickle Jar Communications (a digital communications agency for the education sector).
        
- Utterly Content (working to boost customer engagement through creative content marketing).
        

        
I lead the development of:
        

        
- digital content strategies for websites and social media sites
        
- audience and competitor research and benchmarking reports
        
- content and channel (including social media) plans
        
- recommendations for marketing and communications campaigns 
        
- creative content creation.
        

        
In addition, I also speak at conferences around the world on social media engagement, content strategy and creation, marketing and measurement and evaluation.
        

        
I am supported by an outstanding team but I am always interested in hearing from others who share my enthusiasm, dedication and commitment to quality who may one day also like to join one of my companies, or work across both. 
        

        
Prior to establishing Pickle Jar Communications (and latterly Utterly Content) I worked as the Head of Research-TV. Based at the University of Warwick, I worked on behalf of universities and organisations in the UK and mainland Europe to achieve coverage of research stories on television channels around the world and online.
        

        
I have additional experience of:
        

        
- working in a press office (include proactive media relations and reactive media relations)
        
- stakeholder communications
        
- stakeholder relations
        
- communications planning
        
- online communications and web development
        
- internal communications
        
- change management
        
- event management.
        

        
I am a member of the Institute of Directors (IoD) and a member of the Chartered Institute of Public Relations (CIPR, of which I am also a former Council member).

        Specialties:Digital and Social Media, Broadcast PR, PR for HE and Research sectors, Event Management, Business Development, Copywriting, Communications Strategy, Communications Planning, Workshops and Training.
