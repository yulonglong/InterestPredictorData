      Background
      Summary
      Founding partner of Shippingcluster (www.shippingcluster.com), the maritime social network, exclusively for the global shipping industry, eliminating all unrelated noise, making people and business easy to find and be found! 
      

      
The shipping industry is the most international industry in the world, serving more than 90% of the global trade. The industry is largely based on networking. Surprisingly, it has not had its own social network - until now. 
      

      
Prior, I have founded Circle Innovation, an IT consultancy company, together with two colleagues from Andersen Consulting (now Accenture) and Genus Software, a software applications framework.
      

      
Through ShipNet, where I also met my co-founder Tormod Haavi, I was introduced to the global shipping industry and was fasinated by how the dynamics of this industry resembles the activities in social networks.
      

      
I have a Bachelor of Computer Science from the University of Massachusetts and a Master in Technology Management from NTNU in Norway / the University of Singapore.
      Presentation to the HRH Crown Prince and Princess of Norway in San Francisco, CA on May 9, 2013
