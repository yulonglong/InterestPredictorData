      Background
      Summary
      I am an academic turned freelance science journalist. I write for Indian and international publications on a variety of science topics which mostly circle around ecology and evolution. I have strong research and analytical skills, good people skills and am now experienced working with editors and scientists across time zones and still managing hard deadlines.
      

      
I am also the editor of Research Media Services, a division of Gubbi Labs. Our team plans to help academic institutes connect with the media better and thus reach out to the public.
      The Melting Pot

      Experience
      Chief Editor
      Science Media Centre at IISc
      August 2014 – Present (1 year)Thiruvananthapuram/Bangalore
      We began our operations at IISc formally from 1st August this year and have been sending weekly press releases based on research outputs from the institute (papers published, awards won by scientists and so on). We have established a process where all the releases goes through a dual vetting process, first by the concerned researcher and then by the institution to ensure that the reporting is factually correct and accurate. We have managed about 5 releases a week. As of 20th October 2014, we have sent out about 60 releases, of which about 22 have been picked up different print and internet media, resulting in more than 75 appearances in a span of about 11 weeks.
      Science Media Center @ IISc
      Edit
      Co-founder and Chief Editor
      Research Media Services, Gubbi Labs
      January 2014 – Present (1 year 7 months)On the cloud!
      The idea is to help academic institutes reach out to the public, through the media. We can help write up press releases about academic papers. We also offer other services like providing event coverage for conferences and talks, facilitating outreach through social media, designing research portals, brochures, newsletters and publicity material as appropriate. 
      
Our team has started our first and flagship venture, the Science Media Centre at IISc.
