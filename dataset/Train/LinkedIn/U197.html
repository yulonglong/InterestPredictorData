        Background
        Summary
        My early career was spent in Advertising and Design where I managed many high profile Identity reviews. After moving to web design and establishing some of the earliest “DotComs” at Nettec plc, I founded e-business software company Intershop UK Ltd in 1997 growing that business to 110 people and £10m revenues by 2001. I spent 5 years selling Sharepoint for Msft Gold Partners creating strategy and new business wins for each from scratch. I built a UK territory for Kapow Technologies in 2005 and supported Brand Consultancy Uffindellwest 2006-9. 
        
I started running in 2008 and launched www.therunningbug.co.uk, a social network for runners from which I exited in 2011. 
        
Today I offer business development and social media consultancy on a contract basis. I also script, shoot, edit and distribute short videos of people, places, products and events and I help to get these discovered on social channels. A recent campaign for a non-profit client delivered a 7-fold increase in fundraising revenues.
