        Background
        Summary
        Stacey is renowned for her expressive, instantly recognizable art. Her drawings, paintings, and illustrations are shared with over 7,000 Facebook page followers. (See; World Arabian Horse Association page on Facebook.)
        

        
Stacey's blogs have appeared on; ActiveRain.com, LeadersWest.com, SiteProNews.com and VisionWeb.com.
        

        
Founder of Stacey's Free Online Coloring Books on AWhiteHorse.com™ in 1996, webmaster of BringingLighttoHYPP.org, and original webmaster of WAHO.org, representing registrars of purebred Arabian horses from seventy-four countries. Stacey's artwork has been selected as logo art for organizations and businesses worldwide.
        

        
Stacey has earned a Top 1% on PeerIndex. plus a Klout score of 60+, while working full-time as a part of the outstanding team at Visionaire Eyecare since September, 2013. Applying her talents as a digital illustrator, blogger, and webmaster, has brought appreciable growth to the privately owned practice in Edmonds, WA.
        

        
Award-winning webmaster leveraging digital art experience, creative thinking, and experience for today's small business owners. Early Joomla! CMS webmaster and proponent. Stacey also enjoys creating original and artistic apps. "A fantastic example of what you can do for your business with appsbar."​ - Joe's Appsbar Blog, 2/13/2013.
        

        
Specialties; community engagement, SEO, SEM, illustrator, copy writing, print design, app creation, promotion, business marketing, Joomla!, health industry, blogging, social media marketing, real estate marketing, public speaking, editing, graphic design. 
        

        
Stacey is a Seattle native, artist, and lifetime equestrian.
