        Rob Riggs is a talented project manager with more than 10 years experience developing technical solutions for organizations representing a wide array of industries. Rob leads the Your Design Online development team. He and his team are responsible for taking the concept, the design, the graphics and copy and transforming them into effective digital experiences.
        

        
EXPERIENCE
        
With a background in development, Rob’s business acumen and technical knowledge have proven to be assets when communicating with clients and with the implementation teams he leads. Rob has experience working with Fortune 500 firms as well as small businesses, and he understands how technology is best utilized by both types of organizations. 
        

        
Rob specializes in analyzing and improving business processes and workflow, project management, software implementation, information architecture (IA), application programming interface (API), content management systems (CMS) and document management systems (DMS), search engine optimization and marketing (SEO/SEM), and single sign-on (SSO).
