        Background
        Summary
        Louise Radman is is the founding director of Radpublic. A leading influencer in the field of brand identity and experiential marketing, Louise Radman has over 20 years experience in wine and luxury goods sectors. A distinguished career spanning marketing, public relations, journalism, procurement and executive management has equipped her to master brilliant campaigns. 
        

        
Louise Radman holds a bachelor degree in wine marketing from the University of Adelaide. Louise sits on the brand Tasmania council, the board of directors for wine communicators of Australia and the national executive of sommeliers Australia. Louise is a Roseworthy alumni and Len Evans scholar. She sits on numerous judging panels including Australia's wine list of the year awards, best sommelier of Australia competition, AWRI advanced wine assessment course, London International Wine Challenge and various wine shows. She spent many years in senior department head positions with Hyatt hotels and as a multi-award winning wine writer. 
        

        
Louise has a strong network of relationships at the highest levels of the international wine and media worlds. Her expertise has been called upon by Wine Australia, Tasting Australia, Hyatt, The Langham, The Mayfair, Coca-Cola, Clarendon Hills, Seppeltsfield, Domaine A, AWRI, ASVO, Le Cordon Bleu and Hotellerie Suisse.
        

        
Louise is also a director of Domaine Simha in Tasmania. The avant-garde wine company is founded on pristine terroir at the edge of the world. Breathtaking cool climate wines are shaped 'au naturel' to harness the provenance of terroir and season. They reflect some of the most pure terroir, sparkling air and ancient soil on earth.

        Domaine Simha, Tasmania’s Avant-Garde.
        
The house of Singh originates from ancient sanskrit for lion, Simha. Established by talented vigneron Nav Singh and Louise Radman, Domaine Simha is founded on the pristine terroir of Tasmania. The island paradise at the edge of the world is home to breathtaking cool climate vineyards. Between the mountains and the Great Southern Ocean, they reflect some of the most pure terroir, sparkling air and ancient soil on earth. Wines are harvested with the lunar cycle and shaped au naturel to harness the provenance of terroir and season. An artisan approach employs whole bunch wild fermentation, traditional pigeage, maturation in custom demi-muid oak and perfect natural balance without fining or filtration.
