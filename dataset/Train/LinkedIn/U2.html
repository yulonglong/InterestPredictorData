        Background
        Summary
        I am an entrepreneur with more than 4 years of experience building teams and products in the education, recruitment and manufacturing domains. I am currently working as the Chief Marketing and Operations officer at Examify, an ed-tech start up, disrupting the exam prep space which has acquired more than 30,000 users since inception. 
        

        
Until 2014, I was heading the User Acquisition team at Mauka Technologies which I co-founded. Mauka was a start up which was working on a real time automated recruitment platform for companies to hire undergrads and post grads from universities and was one of the first student startups from Hyderabad to have raised funds of more than USD100,000. 
        

        
I have developed a passion for solving problems with disruptive solutions and in this process have learnt to market myself and the products I dealt with. I have also garnered a special interest in Business Modelling and incubating an idea and have also been certified on the same by infoDev, World Bank. I have been selected to attend the Draper University, San Francisco, where I have strengthened my practical exposure to finance and accounting with theoretical concepts and models. My experience dealing with startups from different domains gives me an edge over others especially while strategizing growth channels and implementing cross platform promotions.
        

        
I am a Global Fellow at the Kairos Society and have been part of the Entrpereneurship Summer School in Belgium where I have been exposed to ideas and entrepreneurs from diverse backgrounds and cultures.
        

        
I have actively campaigned against the adverse effects of Environmental Pollution and Climate Change while volunteering for the Indian Youth Climate Network and also consult non profits and social ventures for their tech and operational issues.
        

        
I am a graduate from BITS Pilani with a dual degree in Biology and Chemical Engineering and am a photography/ creative arts enthusiast and have led the Department of Photography at BITS Pilani.
