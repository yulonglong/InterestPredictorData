        Background
        Summary
        Norman (http://normanvaldez.com) is the Project Director of Vivid Design (http://vividize.me). He brings over a decade of experience as a Web Designer, Graphic Designer and Online Community Manager. He has a Masters in Information Systems and an Honors Bachelors Degree with a specialization in Digital Enterprise Management, both from the University of Toronto and he has taught at the University of Toronto and Sheridan College in the Communication Culture and Information Technology program. Norman has also worked at the Canadian Education and Research Institute for Counselling (CERIC) as the Digital Media and Communications Manager overseeing the websites, social media strategy and cloud based systems. He is an avid blogger and is passionate about a range of topics like technology, social media, politics, health, psychology and philosophy. Norman is the editor of http://newmediawarrior.net.
