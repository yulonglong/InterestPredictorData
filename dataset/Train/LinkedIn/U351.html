        Background
        Summary
        Shortly after graduate school while entrenched in doctoral work in another field of study, my desire for the creative arts was too strong to ignore. Following a successful bout in organizational leadership and consulting, while authoring "A Remarkable Practice", I spent the following years refining my trade as a designer and photographer. I currently lead my two businesses, serving numerous roles from creative lead and designer to photographer and project lead. I am best known for a timeless, professional design aesthetic and an ability to push the limits of photography. I’ve garnered success by winning awards as both a mixed-media designer & photographer.
