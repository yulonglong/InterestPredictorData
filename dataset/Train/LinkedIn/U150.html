        Background
        Summary
        Knowing that the customer experience is the sum of all parts I focus on an integrated approach of technology, live interactions, data, marketing/communications, lifecycle engagement and collaboration, both internally and externally, to build a stronger online community where community members gain value from each other and from the entity where the overall affinity originates.
        

        
My 20 years of diverse experience in social media, data integration, business intelligence, marketing/communication, corporate IT, consulting, higher education, alumni relations, small business, startup, strategy, execution, never ending curiosity and desire to learn allow me to succeed in individual engagement on a mass scale by applying both art and science to:
        

        
*Inform and inspire by creating and sharing well branded, emotionally relevant and strategically informed content
        
*Foster personal and professional networking opportunities
        
*Cultivate learning, enrichment and career opportunities by leveraging channels across campus
        
*Connect people to people, content to people, people through content
        
*Extend the campus experience - academics, campus life, athletics, nostalgia, chapters
        
*Recognize alumni and students for their achievements around the world for all to see
        
*Help alumni and students feel a sense of ownership/contribution by answering questions, solving problems
        

        
My favorite current social platform is Twitter and I consider myself well versed in using all the main social platforms from both and organizational and personal perspective. I am also familiar with the leading online community platforms.

        Specialties:I bring a systematic perspective to community management. I bring value at the strategic and day to day level in both front end interaction points and back end data, systems, technologies, processes and organizational culture that all impact the engagement experience.
