        Background
        Summary
        Professional speaker, consultant and published author, Fred Colantonio leads humans in brands, companies and entrepreneurs to create, grow and deal with their best business attitude and corporate brand.
        

        
"An expert who makes things more complicated isn't doing their job right (…) An expert should make things simpler."
        
Richard Branson, Virgin brand founder
        

        
Criminologist specialized in New Media and Technologies, Fred Colantonio used his habilities to understand the mindset and behavior of an individual as part of a group to serve growth in various business contexts. Nowadays, his approach encompasses a vision of human potential with "L’attitude des Héros" (www.lattitudedesheros.com and www.facebook.com/lattitudedesheros).
        

        
He gives inspirational talks in French and English mostly in Europe (so far: Belgium, Luxembourg, France, Switzerland, Greece, Portugal, Croatia).

Steve Jobs, Apple co-founder

        Experience
        Professional speaker, mentor and published author
        L'attitude des Héros
        2012 – Present (3 years)Europe and Worldwide
        L'attitude des Héros ® started with a conference. The aim was to demonstrate one simple point : we can all chose to succeed in business as in life. 
        

        
To get people there, I used reseaches I've done for 5 years about people whose success is international and indeniable, people from the business world : Steve Jobs, Richard Branson, Oprah Winfrey, Paulo Coelho, Ingvar Kamprad.
        

        
I defined the similar patterns they all share in their attitude and course of life to get where they are now. L'attitude des Héros demonstrates how close they are to what everyone encounters in life. Therefore, success is all about attitude and choice.
        

        
This talk changed everything : from one speech I thought I'd be giving once, L'attitude des Héros has become something huge in my own business and my own life. Nowadays, it is : 
        

        
3 great talks : Inspiration, Action and Signification.
        
3 books : Inspiration, Action and Signification
        

        
L'attitude des Héros ® is also a publishing house : it prints and distributes its own books.
        

        
Alongside with the great talks, I work with leaders, managers and ambitious entrepreneurs to stimulate their best winning attitude, lead them to excel and give them what they want to do better and go beyond what they tought was possible for them.
