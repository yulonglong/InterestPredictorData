        I'm a a filmmaker and digital artist. Some call me an art director, some call me a filmmaker or a videomaker, some call me an artist. 
        
The truth is that I work in both fields, and I try to combine them whenever I can. 
        
Usually I write, budget, produce, and even film. I work solo or in teams, usually for international projects, compelling, engaging pieces for TV, web, you name it. I was lucky enough to learn from and with the best, so I can deliver high quality products.
        

        
That’s what I do.
