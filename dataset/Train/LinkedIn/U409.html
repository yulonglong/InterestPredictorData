        Background
        Summary
        I am a Frontend Web Developer/Web Designer with experience in Joomla, Drupal and Wordpress websites. My job experience and freelance work have given me a very broad range of exciting projects to work on. Projects have ranged from small, static, html and css websites to large, dynamic, thousand page websites built on content management systems like Joomla. I have created custom Joomla templates and designed and built many Joomla websites. Joomla makes it easy for a layperson to have control over the updates to their site. It's great to be able to give that freedom to clients and it frees me up to make larger changes for them having to do with functionality.
        

        
I stay current in Web Design by completing countless tutorials and webinars online. I also attend great conferences like the CMS Expo in Chicago and Adobe Max to stay on top of the latest happenings in web. Web design and development is constantly changing and that makes me incredibly excited to be a Web Designer in Baltimore.
