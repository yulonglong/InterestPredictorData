        July 2011 – March 2015 (3 years 9 months)Houston, Texas Area
        Social Media is easy to pass off as a fad. Or at least it was. When it first came out most people thought it wouldn’t be able to convert real dollars. So they ignored it. Or they tried it but didn’t stick with it because they weren’t getting results.
        

        
Everyone was putting their money in SEO and they still should be. But it was different a few years ago. Heck, it was different a few months ago! Google rolls out changes to their algorithm at speeds that can give you whiplash.
        

        
So the “smart money” was to work with Adwords or other PPC campaigns and let the SEO guys try to outsmart Google. 
        

        
That doesn’t work much longer. Google has evolved. Customers have evolved. Their buying habits have changed.
        

        
SEO and Social Media are merging. That’s Modern Marketing for you. 
        

        
With our forward thinking marketing consulting you’ll be able to streamline your efforts with modern marketing strategies and get back to working on your business and taking care of your customers.
        

        
One of my retail clients saw a growth of over 2300% on Twitter and they’re getting more and more customers inquiries from this previously dead space for them. 
        

        
Now we’re working on getting their website properly optimized on the SEO side of things and the results are already taking shape. 
        

        
Learn more about how Social Media and Search Engine Optimization are just a small part of the equation at: http://damienfranco.com/blog/
