Welcome to My Profile...
        

        
I'm passionate about 3 things: 
        

        
1. My family - my mother, sister, my girlfriend Jess and my dogs Oscar & Samwise. 
        

        
2. Helping others realise their own and their businesses potential through my invest, consume and act model for success and 
        

        
3. Direct response marketing and lead generation for business owners and entrepreneurs through marketing consulting.
        

        
I am the author of the highly acclaimed book 'Your Elephant's Under Threat'. It has received worldwide acclaim from some of the top experts and individuals across the globe. To learn more about me or to get my book visit http://JohnMulry.com
        

        
I am also Ireland's only GKIC Certified Business Advisor and was hand picked and trained under Dan Kennedy to bring his and GKIC style marketing and lead generation into Ireland. I conduct a monthly marketing and business building summit in the Ballybane Enterprise Centre in Galway, Ireland.
        

        
This is Who I Help:
        

        
As a freelance marketing consultant, lead generation and client experience specialist, I consult with and show business owners and entrepreneurial professionals how to attract more clients for their business, how to give them an amazing experience which results in them staying and referring.
        

        
This is how I do it:
        

        
I started the John Mulry freelance marketing consultancy business because there's a massive gap in knowledge in business. My marketing consultancy business was created to provide business owners with the tools, resources and support they need to accomplish their goals. How? First, we help you target your ideal client, understand exactly what they want when they make their decision to buy, and create compelling marketing messages that enable you to out-market your competition. We even provide you with "done-for-you" lead generation,marketing and advertising materials that are proven and tested to get real-world results. 
        

        
Visit http://JohnMulry.com for marketing consulting

2.
