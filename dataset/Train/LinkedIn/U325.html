        Summary
        Business Ghostwriting is my specialty. I have written in a wide range of niche business areas for over 20 years. My process works and those who have taken advantage of my expertise will testify to that fact. 
        

        
Here is the question that prospective clients ask me: What do you do and WHAT can you do for me?
        

        
Here is my answer:
        

        
I can educate and persuade your clients through the written word to buy your products or services. I can do this through creating a book, an eBook, articles, blog strategies, website content, press releases, and any other format you might dream up. 
        

        
I can do this because I have years of experience as a business writer, and an entrepreneur. I can do this because I want to help you elevate your bottom line with the skill I have acquired through a lifetime of working. 
        

        
Contact me at aprilsmythe@gmail.com and appoint me to be your business writer!
