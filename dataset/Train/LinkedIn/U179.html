        Background
        Summary
        Christy Belden has worked in marketing for over 12 years. She has worked specifically in digital marketing for the last 8 years.
        

        
Christy's current position as Digital Director t for Courier-Journal Media, a Gannett company. Using data and analytics to lead her decision making, she works in creating and implementing integrated digital marketing solutions for clients. Christy also assist in raising the awareness of digital knowledge and expertise within Louisville.
        

        
In her past roles, Christy has worked with clients on all of their digital marketing needs: SEO, PPC, Social Media, Digital Media, e-Commerce/e-Business Marketing, Email Marketing and Mobile Marketing. She has also run campaigns in traditional media channels: Radio, Print, Television, Out-of-Home and Movie Theater advertising. 
        

        
Christy is an adjunct professor at Spalding University where she teaches courses on digital marketing. 
        

        
Christy Belden speaks on digital marketing. She has also been published in several trade publications on the subject. 
        

        
Specialties: SEO, SEM, PPC, Mobile Search, B2B Marketing, Market Research, HTML, Website Analytics, Branding, Client Strategists, Print Media, Event Marketing, Display Media, Social Media, Email Marketing, e-Commerce/e-Business Marketing, Mobile Marketing, Lead Generation, Demand Generation, Small Business Marketing
