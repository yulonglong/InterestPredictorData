        Summary
        Move, Touch and Inspire others through How You're Being, to Do what you can, to Have what you want.
        

        
A leading expert in CONNECTING WITHOUT COMPROMISE, Thomas' belief is that extraordinary relationships are about bold, courageous conversations moving from the ordinary small talk to conversation that truly makes a difference.
        

        
Coming from a family of entrepreneurs, Thomas has experienced from the very earliest age what works in the world business. 
        

        
Drawing on a rich and diverse 31 year + experience as a military interrogator, corporate consultant, entrepreneur & radio show host, Thomas helps teams “connect” so that it creates tangible results in their business and personal life. i.e more time for what matters most.
        

        
Operating his first brick and mortar business at the age of 20 along with many home-based businesses earlier than that, Thomas learned quickly that success comes from the strategic relationships that are cultivated through meaningful conversation. 
        

        
Thomas’ first book, working title “Love The One You’re With" is in the works.
