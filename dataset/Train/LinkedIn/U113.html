        Choose Well Now
        January 2004 – Present (11 years 7 months)
        I've been an entrepreneur all my life. At 27 I started my first business: Select Hosting, providing staffing to companies for trade shows. My next venture was an Import business, working with high end leather goods. 
        

        
I've worked as a flight attend for a major airline for over 33 years & had a growing concern about my financial future. I grew tired of trading time for money & was looking for a Plan B or an exit strategy.
        

        
I found those answers in the Network Marketing Industry, where I worked my business part time, with a 5 year goal to retire myself early from my airline career. 
        

        
In 5 and a half years, I rose to become one of the Top 50 income earners in the company &, became their 40th millionaire, by helping others fulfil their goals & dreams. I am happy to say that I am now retired & ‘living life by design’ 
        

        
My husband, Steve and I have developed an incredible team, helping 8 people earn a multiple 6 figure income and 1 become a millionaire. 
        

        
I've discovered the secret to making money on the Internet and now have the privilege to teach others how to successfully start their own business.
        

        
I believe that every human being deserves to live to their fullest potential. Every day I partner with a Team of Visionaries, who empower others to 'design the life they want'
