        Background
        Summary
        I am a fifteen year business and IT executive with deep experience in consulting, data analysis, enterprise strategy, and project management (former PMP). I also have an MBA education and an undergraduate degree in Mathematics giving me a unique and powerful combination of business savory and technical expertise.
        

        
Recently I have turned my focus to the creation of Nerdies, Arties and the Foundation for Nerd Advancement. I set out to find a place where my kids could go and pursue the things that they love. As I searched I realized there just wasn’t a place for my kids or any other smart kids out there who are into different things besides sports and music lessons. Nor was there a place for them to be around other smart kids in a social environment. From this desire came Nerdies (launched in June 2014) and Arties (launched August 2014).

        Founder and Head Nerd In Charge
        Nerdies
        July 2013 – Present (2 years 1 month)Fayetteville, Arkansas Area
        Nerdies focuses on all us nerds out there by offering a unique environment where people of all ages come and pursue the things that they are interested in and love. Whether that is participating in a technology class, getting individualized tutoring, playing video games with friends or just hanging out in the Nerdies Caffeine Café, Nerdies provides a unique environment for all those people who think ‘smart is cool’ and are interested in pursuing activities they enjoy in this new tech world.
        

        
In August of 2014 we also launched a new brand called Arties. As a subsidiary of Nerdies, Arties was specifically designed to concentrate on the creative side of life and the incredible people who inhabit that space. We felt like there needed to be a brand, an identity, and focus specifically geared toward this unique population and out of that came Arties. 
        

        
Arties is built for the artist that is ready to do and in doing wanting to learn and do more. We focus on outcome based learning by simply doing it. Movies don't get made by talking about them. Music isn't made by talking. Bands aren't created, comic books aren't written, inspiring photography isn't taken, by talking. At Arties, we do all of this and more.
