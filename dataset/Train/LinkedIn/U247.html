      I am 3D Architectural Visualizer looking for New opportunities in the Animation industry.

      I am graduated in B.Sc(Animation & Gaming ) from Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University, Pune, Maharashtra, India.

      I also work as a Freelancer 3D Architectural Visualizer working online for clients. Primarily I have taken on Design Visualization projects, as well as some architectural drawings, and 3D and 2D Component modeling/design for production.

I am always working to improve my workflow and design practices to make better products. I have a very methodical and practical approach to design, and a very analytical mind. I am proficient in 3DS max, Maya, Photoshop, illustrator and AutoCAD.
