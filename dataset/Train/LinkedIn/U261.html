        Background
        Summary
        I'm a Portuguese designer, with a degree in Technology and Graphic Arts and 15 years of work experience in the area of Graphic Design, Graphic Production and Web Design.
        
I seek to establish long-term relationships with challenging companies that will allow me to use my experience and that can also provide me the tools to grow as a professional. 
        
The range of my work goes from corporate design (logos, stationary, signage, uniforms, vehicles, merchandising, etc.), packaging (food and non food), editorial (desktop publishing, books, magazines, etc.), up to the design of graphic elements such as outdoors, posters, triptychs, brochures, flyers, etc., and also through the web design (creating layouts for websites and applications).
        
My ultimate goal is to continuously improve my skills and knowledge in order to be a better designer everyday.
