        Summary
        Martin Krammer shows 20 years of working experience as an architect in architectural practices in the USA and Europe. He founded and owns KMDcc - Organization and Communication for Architecture and Design providing services in project and strategy development, organization and communications for architects, designers, communities and public institutions. 
        
He is co-founder and owner of architecture-export.com. Preparing architecture projects for the media, offering efficient data management and an international online press service.
        

        
Martin Krammer currently works as Head of Business Development for Switzerland's leading wood construction company ERNE AG Holzbau, i.a. specialized in modular elements for health and education buildings, free form parametric design and constructions, window manufacturing, etc.
