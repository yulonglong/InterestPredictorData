        • Why is it hard to buy your services?
          June 23, 2014
        


        Background
        Summary
        Judith Germain, leading authority on Reputational Leadership and mavericks, is the originator of the advanced leadership concept Reputational Leadership and she helps individuals & organisations develop mastery in this area.
        

        
She is the Principal Consultant of Seeing Solutions & will ensure that you are provided with a bespoke solution to help YOU harness maverick talent to succeed in business. 
        

        
Judith is particularly well known in the industry for working with wilfully independent people who are talented & different (whom she defines as Mavericks). 
        

        
Her mentoring & training programmes, consultancy & keynote speeches, centre around enabling organisations & mavericks to develop reputational leadership, harness their willful independence, build their reputation and improve their communication and interaction with others. 
        

        
She explains how to improve performance by harnessing the creative tension of mavericks or Troublesome Talent® and effectively managing talent and motivation. Judith shows individuals and organisations how to demonstrate true leadership & leverage their reputation & influence to ‘get what they want’ & achieve real change & impressive results.
        

        
Having taught over 100 businesses how to improve their reputation & credibility, her Leadership dRive™ model challenges audiences to consider the impact of their reputation & influence on the performance of the business and the impact on those around them.
        

        
She can also demonstrate how your Social Media Strategy can lead & influence online & demonstrate your expertise, therefore building advocacy, loyal customers & improving reputation for you & your organisation. As a direct result of her expertise, business owners have seen their revenue double through adopting her smarter business strategies. 
        

        
Her expert opinion has appeared in numerous publications including national broadsheets and leading industry press. She is presently authoring her new book on mavericks.

        Specialties:Reputational leadership and working with mavericks.
        

        
Speaking, Maverick Mentoring, Mavericks, Leadership Development, Leadership training, Talent management, reputation management, harnessing Troublesome Talent®, Graduate development, strategy development, social networking, Business and executive coaching, training, management development training, change management, Organizational Development. Business Strategy,strategic management, strategic and operational HR Management, Branding
        Experience
        Principal Consultant
        Seeing Solutions Management Services Ltd
        July 2013 – Present (2 years 1 month)
        Seeing Solutions in an ever shifting landscape isn't easy. Finding your place in the world has become increasingly difficult as this century gets older. The old staples are no longer stable and we are no longer certain of the old certainties. Things have to change and the time for a new type of leader is upon us.
        

        
Not only do we expect individuals to lead, we insist that the companies that 'serve us' also show true leadership as well. Not 20th Century leadership either, a new type of leadership made perfect for this Century. A time when reputation (integrity and capability) dominates, social intelligence is the norm and a maverick streak is essential! (Judith Germain describes the term 'maverick' as wilful independence).
        

        
Reputational leadership (which has been defined and developed by Judith Germain to mean leading through influence) is ideal for these times. Winning companies and inspiring and productive individuals are able to influence customers and employees to the benefit of the business.
        

        
In these turbulent times whoever can build a reputation for seeing solutions within difficult or complex scenarios and be able to implement them with the least amount of fuss will beat their competition and improve their own professional standing.
        

        
We have solutions for:
        

        
* Consultants and Small Businesses
        
* Larger and Corporate Businesses
        
* Those requiring a Professional Speaker
        

        
We help individuals and businesses with:
        

        
* Defining and establishing their value proposition, build and leverage their reputation and increase their revenue and attraction.
        
* Transforming their business through Reputational Leadership
        
* Maverick Mentoring
        
* Improving employee performance
        
* Strategic leadership
        
* How to be more influential
        
* CEO and executive mentoring 
        

        
We help YOU manage maverick talent to SUCCEED in business. Stand out from your competitors to make a real difference to your business & professional standing.
