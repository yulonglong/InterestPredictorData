        Background
        Summary
        I am visual artist living and working in a small city in the Portuguese west coast. Since 2002 I have been doing both digital and analog works for clients and art projects, mostly culturally focused, under a wide variety of means, formats or mediums.
        

        
I have a passion for figurative representations and grid compositions, sometimes organically unbalanced, sometimes made from spontaneously organised thoughts and words. At the present, I'm working in user interface/experience, as well as illustration concepts allied in different fields, such as the collage project "fitacola" (be.net/fitacola). 
        

        
I am always eager to learn more and be challenged with new projects, ideas and methods, always using my visual and technique experience to intrigue a spectator or solve people's problems.
        

        
Since 2002 I have contributed with interpretations and illustrations for editorials like Flaunt Magazine, The New York Times, among others. My work has been featured by several of Portugal's leading magazines and newspapers (such as Visão magazine or Expresso weekly newspaper) and in other publications worldwide. Also, I have made works for Nike, Los Angeles Museum of Contemporary Art, R.J.Reynolds (Camel), Seat (cars), Ssang Young, Optimus Mobile, and other Portuguese and international brands, products and companies. I also exhibited at CCCB Barcelona, several other galleries, institutions and 'underground' spots across the American, Asian and European continents, as well as in many fine-art events in Portugal.
        

        
My specialties are: Illustration, drawing, painting, collage, Graphic design, User Interfaces, Poster design, Packaging, Apparel, Print Design, Pre-print, Publishing, Portraiture, Environments, Murals, Urban scenes, and I also code some chunks of html and css for personal web projects.
        

        
Lately I have been deeply involved in the startup industry, trying to figure out what will be the best way to change the world with a a global engaging platform for the arts and culture.
