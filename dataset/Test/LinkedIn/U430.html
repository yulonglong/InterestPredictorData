        Background
        Summary
        Eliot has worked in both agency and client-side marketing roles, specifically in the sports, entertainment, hospitality and non-profit industries. Former roles include Social Media Intern at Canucks Sports & Entertainment, Assistant Coordinator for a national marketing agency, Communications Officer for the University Students' Council at the University of Western Ontario, Editor-in-Chief for an online lifestyle magazine and Operations Manager at an event management company.
        

        
Eliot has direct working experience in management, traditional & digital marketing, social media, event planning, communications strategy, social networking, crisis communications and policy development.
        

        
Eliot is constantly aspiring to grow his career in the field of marketing, and is always excited to take the next step in his journey.
        

        
Eliot enjoys cooking, sports, reading and hitting the gym. He occasionally takes up photography and enjoys a good sunset or two.
        

        
Specialties: New media, digital marketing, event planning, blogging, social media, policy development, communications, branding, marketing, social networking development, HTML, CMS, Microsoft Office, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Premiere Pro, research, project management
