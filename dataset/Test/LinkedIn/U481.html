        Background
        Summary
        I have completed several experiences in three different fields: Journalism, Book Publishing and Academic Education. Now I am a contributor at Tgcom24.mediaset.it and LinC, and I am Coordinator of the Creative Writing Course at Catholic University. Remedia.tgcom24.it is my blog about journalism.
        

        
In the past I worked as a Journalist for Linkiesta.it, Lettera43.it, Style.it, Sky TG24 and as a Journalist and Anchorwoman for Class CNBC and furthermore for the National daily newspaper Libero.
        

        
My role as editor and writer firstly at Musa Editing, secondly at La Vita Felice, Itaca and finally at Piemme testifyies my passion for the publishing and my competences with regard to book projects. 
        

        
Furthermore, as Coordinator of the Creative Writing Course at Catholic University I developed not only my management and teaching skills, but also the capability to organize cultural events.
