        Background
        Summary
        Social media marketing can be quite time consuming. That is why people from a broad range of industries utilize my expertise so they can focus on running their businesses. Additionally, if you don't fully understand how social marketing works you could damage your brand instead of increasing your brand awareness. 
        

        
I have a proven social media marketing strategy to effectively market brands on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Google+, YouTube and etc.
        

        
Step 1:
        
I get to know your business and your industry: What do you do? Who are your competitors? Who’s your target audience? What do you want to accomplish?
        

        
Step 2: 
        
I create an effective marketing campaign that will outline which social media platforms should be utilized to target your audience and accomplish your marketing goals.
        

        
Step 3:
        
I fully optimize your social media accounts on Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn. Plus check and revise your company’s bios, ‘about us’ information, interests, and groups. Essentially, I make sure things are in excellent shape and ready to go.
        

        
Step 4:
        
I establish, build, and effectively maintain your company’s presence on Facebook, Twitter, and any social media platform that is right for your campaign. I post daily updates, establish your expertise, build relationships, increase your influence, respond to comments, and monitor the entire web for mentions of your brand. Additionally I utilize keywords and contextual linking to increase your rankings in Google, Yahoo and Bing.
        

        
Step 5:
        
I analyze conversion rates, make adjustments, and regularly measure progress. I will work with you throughout the entire process to create the results you desire.
