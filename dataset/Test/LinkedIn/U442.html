        Background
        Summary
        Hernán Caamaño (Argentina, 42 years) holds a bachelor´s degree in Communication Sciences (University of Buenos Aires), with postgraduate studies in Cultural Management and Communication (Flacso Argentina) and Descentralized Cooperation for Latin America (UOC).
        
Hernán began his career as a Technical Assistant at the Ibero-American Cooperation Secretariat (SECIB), an intergovernmental organization acting as a Director of the first Ibero-American portal on the Internet, Ciberamerica.org.
        
Later, worked as a Senior ICTs for development Adviser of Secretary General.
        
He currently serves as Head of Project of Citizenship 2.0 (Ciudadania20.org), an initiative developed by the Ibero-American General Secretariat with focus on civic participation and citizen innovation.
        
Before joining the SEGIB, Hernán previously worked as a graphic designer and Strategic Communications consultant for various companies and organizations.
