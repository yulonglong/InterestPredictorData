        Are you looking for IT Outsourcing Services and Computer Support in Greece?
        
Your search is over. We solve your problem by providing a network of 150 points of technical support all around Greece. 
        

        
Have you lost your data or looking for quality Data Recovery Training for your new Data Recovery Lab? We can help you!
        

        
>> Data Recovery Instructor:
        
Teaching the basics of Data Recovery to new Engineers that are coming into the field of Data Recovery. Providing on-site or remote seminars on Data Recovery: Hard disk Structure, Hard disk repairs, Tutorials on specific Tools such as PC3k,Deepspar,SD Doctors, DFL Tools,RStudio,Raid Recovery, PCB Repairs, Head Swaps, Platter Swaps, Hard disk parts compatibility. In the last 3 years I've helped 4 Technical Support companies grow their Data Recovery business around the world. I've personally trained more than 6 Data Recovery engineers start their own business on data recovery.
        

        
>> Data Recovery Engineering background: 
        
Speciality in recovering Seagate,WD, Hitachi and Fujitsu Siemens hard disks with physical or logical problems. Also decoding and recovering NAND chips and SSD Hard disks. RAID recovery from Windows/Linux.
        

        
>> Computer Forensics Field:
        
Computer Forensics Analysis. Worked with various computer forensics cases in the private sector and provided electronic evidence for court. 
        

        
IT Project Management and IT Outsourcing in Greece:
        
Managing European IT Projects, IT Startup Companies and Implementation of new products and services to the market.
