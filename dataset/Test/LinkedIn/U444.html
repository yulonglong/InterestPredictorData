        Background
        Summary
        I am currently responsible for developing, maintaining, and monitoring Spectrum Health System's overall social media presence. This includes working with the system communications and marketing team to develop successful social media marketing initiatives. Additionally, I lead and manage LinkedUp Grand Rapids, a business social networking group of nearly 14,000 members based in the Greater Grand Rapids, West Michigan region.
        
I have a broad background that includes consulting as a social media strategist specializing in Social Media Strategy, Social Media Implementation, Social Media Monitoring, Social Media Marketing, Communications Consulting, Business Development and HR.
        
My experience includes 6+ years of work in most social media channels / social media platforms doing consulting and developing social media strategy for companies in West Michigan and the upper Midwest. It also includes several years of Business Development, Human Resources, Recruitment, Staffing, Vendor Managed Services, Contingent Workforce Management, and Organizational Communications.
        
I have worked with companies of all shapes and sizes from small, fast growing start-ups to large, multi billion dollar corporations (headquarters location). Industries include healthcare, telecommunications, retail (corporate), IT, Scientific, and domestic and international non profit.
        

        
Specialties: Social Media Consulting; Social Media Strategy; Social Media Training; Social Media Marketing, Business Development using Social Media; Staffing using Social Media; Contract Negotiation; Vendor Managed Services; Business Consulting; Social Media Policy; Twitter; LinkedIn, Facebook; YouTube; Blogs; Foursquare; forums; Pinterest; Instagram; MySpace
        

        
Misspellings of my name: Yodder
