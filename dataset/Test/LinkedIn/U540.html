        Summary
        I am a librarian, web developer, content strategist, and educator who frequently teaches and presents on social media and emerging technology tools and trends.
        

        
My research specializations focus on the social web, digital history, Orange County history, and genealogy. My web design specializations focus on information architecture, metadata, APIs, and social media integration . 
        

        
I am looking to network with fellow professionals who share my interests in emerging web technologies, elearning, knowledge management, archival technologies, digital history, genealogy, and marketing for libraries and heritage institutions.
